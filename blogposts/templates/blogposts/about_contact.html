About Blogish

Thank you for viewing Blogish, a simple blogging app, created with Django! Users of the app can create an account* in order to create, edit, and delete posts. Users who do not wish to do any of these actions can simply view the currently available posts.

*The registration form requires an email to be entered, however, there is no email confirmation. A random string of letters (ex: aaa@bbb.cc) will suffice.
